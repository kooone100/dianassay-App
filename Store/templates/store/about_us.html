Who We Are

Dianassay Properties was established in 2013 as a real estate sales company. we couple our expertise in large-scale mixed-use properties, commitment to design innovation, quality and efficiency, and local market insight, to bring or vision to life. To date we have invested a lot in our local projects. At Dianassay Properties, we develop residential and retail properties that create urban communities and accentuate the benefits of modern living. Our properties effortlessly fit within a city, contribute to the local economy and accelerate the development of the surrounding areas. Dianassay Properties prides itself on developing properties that transform communities. Working with the best architects and designers, we are able to create projects that make an everlasting impacting not only within the local communities, but within the cities themselves. With over 7 years' experience in the real estate industry, we use our expertise to create urban destinations. Featuring international design principles and the latest in modern and innovative architecture, we develop unparalleled properties that become integral to each neighbourhood. Each project contributes to the ever-changing skylines in the cities.

Our Vision

Dianassay Properties strives to create vibrant urban communities that change the landscape of their environment.

Our Mission

With Dianassay Properties goal to make "your comfort, our priority", we approach each project with an emphasis on sustainability for both residents and the environments. Each community is constructed to reduce its impact on the local environment while ensuring that it provides the latest in integrated environmental benefits.
